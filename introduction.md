## Full Description

Transform your new tab into a beautiful, organized workspace! HomeBase replaces Chrome's default new tab with a customizable bookmark manager featuring drag-and-drop organization, 5 stunning themes, real-time search, and full accessibility support.

## Key Features

- Smart bookmark organization with custom categories
- Five themes (Elegant Midnight, Modern Tech, Soft Pastel & more)
- Instant search across all your links and categories
- Drag & drop to reorder and organize effortlessly
- Responsive design that works on any screen size
- Full keyboard navigation and screen reader support
- Reliable local storage that never loses your bookmarks

Perfect for professionals, students, and anyone who wants to stay organized and productive. Your bookmarks are safely stored locally and sync across your Chrome browsers.
